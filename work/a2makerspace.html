About A2Makerspace
During the summer of 2023, I was a website design researcher for the Tufts Center for Engineering Education and Outreach. I worked collaboratively with PhD student Kat Allen to design and implement a WCAG 2 AAA compliant website to serve as a "digital makerspace" and collaboration center for accessibility technology.
