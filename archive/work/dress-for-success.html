About
Dress for Success is a global not-for-profit organization that empowers women to achieve economic independence by providing a network of support, professional attire and the development tools to help women thrive in work and in life. As a part of Jumbocode, a student-led organization that creates software for non-profit organizations, I am working alongside a team of developers to create an admin-only web application that keeps track of Dress for Success’ overall inventory of clothing items.
Features
Inventory
  • Displays list of items by quantity, size, article of clothing
    • When a clothing item is entered, include color, SKU number
    • Quantity decreases when item taken out by tracker
    • Quantity increases when items are added
  • Input/search by SKU number
    • Can sort by quantity, size, article of clothing
    • Filterable - “5 medium shirts”, “4 small pants”
Tracker
  • Descriptions of items taken out (i.e. article of clothing, size, SKU number, color)
  • When an item is taken out, a specific item (by SKU, etc.) is taken out, not just decrementing one of the category
  • Date it was taken out
Add Donation Items
  • Updates quantity in inventory with newly donated/added items
  • Input SKU number, then input article of clothing and size
  • Date it was taken out
Remove Items
  • Items can be recategorized as donation, sale, or damage
  • Donation
    • Items given to clients
    • Decreases quantity in inventory when permanently removing an item
    • Input SKU number
  • Sale
    • Items that are past season and can be sold to clients
    • Decreases quantity in inventory when permanently removing an item
    • Input SKU number
  • Damage/Remove
    • Items that are damaged or removed for another reason
    • Decreases quantity in inventory when permanently removing an item
    • Input SKU number
Schema
Clothing items are categorized into distinct groups, each featuring its own size and type:
  • Outerwear
  • Cardigans/Sweaters
  • Jackets/Blazers
  • Suits
  • Scrubs
  • Tops
  • Pants
  • Dresses/Skirts
  • Innerwear
  • Shoes
  • Accessories
